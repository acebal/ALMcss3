Intrinsic Minimum and Intrinsic Preferred Widths

This is an example document to test how intrinsic minimum and intrinsic preferred widths can be calculated.

Specifically, some examples are provided to see how other CSS layout contexts (tables, floats…) compute their width based on their contents, and how those widths are affected by the presence of horizontal margins and padding, and vertical borders on such content elements.
